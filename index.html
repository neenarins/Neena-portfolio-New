About me

Hello there! My name is Neena and I am an aspiring front-end developer. As someone who thoroughly enjoys feeling intellectually stimulated and challenged, I enrolled in Rutgers University Coding Bootcamp, where I was taught a wide range of developer tools, and languages, including, but not limited to, Git, HTML/CSS, BootStrap, JavaScript, jQuery, Node, Express, MySql, and MongoDb. However, though I essentially learned what is needed in order to be a back-end developer, I found that I feel the most challenged and fulfilled working as a front-end developer, and wish to continue creating user-friendly sites, ultimately advancing my knowledge and skills in this area.

Student
